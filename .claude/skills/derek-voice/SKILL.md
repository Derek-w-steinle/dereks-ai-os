# Derek's writing voice

Apply O7's voice to any draft. Read the samples first, then match cadence and
word choice rather than imposing a generic "marketing" tone.

## Read the samples first

Read `docs/writing-samples.md` for reference passages. If it is missing, ask the
user to paste two or three representative samples and offer to save them there.

## Voice rules

- Prefer plain, concrete sentences over jargon and hype.
- Prefer active voice and a direct opener over throat-clearing.
- Prefer specific nouns and numbers over vague claims.
- Keep one idea per sentence; vary sentence length for rhythm.

## Checklist before returning copy

- Does it sound like the samples read aloud?
- Are the claims specific and verifiable?
- Is every sentence carrying weight?
